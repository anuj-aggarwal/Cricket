		PLAY


	Asks for the two teams which will play the next match
Rules:
	- Total Overs: 10
	- Total wickets: 5
	- Maximum Overs a Player can bowl: 3

Toss:
	- First team has the HEADS, second has the TAILS



In the version of the game, there is only one batsman playing i.e. Batsman on strike.
Both teams will get full batting untill either all their batsmen are out or all the balls are over.
The result of a match is decided on the basis of the total runs scored by the two teams

All names are "Case Sensitive"
